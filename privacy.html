Privacy Policy

Last updated November 23, 2023.

This policy explains what personal information Smart Checkout Widgets collects, how it is used, and what rights users have regarding access, correction, deletion, and privacy requests.

What information we collect

We may collect contact details such as name and email address, device and browser information, cookie-related information, and other information submitted through our service or support interactions.

How information is used

  • To provide and improve the Smart Checkout Widgets service.
  • To process transactions and support requests.
  • To communicate product, policy, or operational updates.
  • To protect the service against abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access.

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and related storage technologies to understand site usage, support functionality, and measure marketing performance. Browser settings can be used to limit or disable cookies, though some features may work less effectively.

Data retention and security

We retain information only for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

User rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection to certain processing. We also describe GDPR and California privacy rights where applicable.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to admin@thoughtbulb.dev.
