Shopify checkout gift apps compared (2026)

Updated May 20, 2026 Smart Checkout Widgets All guides

Shopify checkout gift apps let merchants show free gifts, BOGO rewards, or shopper-selected rewards inside checkout (on Shopify Plus) or on Thank You / Order Status surfaces. This page compares evaluation criteria and documents Smart Checkout Widgets — verify any other app on its public Shopify App Store listing before you buy.

Feature comparison

Capability Smart Checkout Widgets Typical checkout gift apps
Automatic threshold gifts in checkout Yes (Plus checkout UI) Varies — confirm on listing
Customer-choice gift selection Yes Some apps offer picker UI; others fixed SKU only
Checkout upsells Yes, same extension Often a separate upsell app
BOGO / automatic discounts in checkout Yes Varies; may require Shopify Functions stack
Trust badges & payment customization Yes Rare in gift-only apps
Thank You / Order Status content Yes (all Shopify stores) Varies
Starter pricing Free to install (see pricing.md) Check each App Store page

Best for

  • Smart Checkout Widgets — Shopify Plus teams that want gifting, upsells, discounts, and trust content in one checkout extension without stitching multiple single-purpose apps.
  • Gift-only apps — Merchants who only need a single fixed gift SKU and do not plan to merchandise upsells or payment methods in the same flow.
  • Post-purchase upsell tools — Brands optimizing after payment; pair carefully with checkout gifts to avoid duplicate offers (see Thank You guide).

How to evaluate any app

  1. Confirm Shopify Plus requirement for checkout UI vs Thank You-only widgets.
  2. Run a test order on mobile — gifts should appear before pay, not only on the thank-you page.
  3. Check whether promotions are automatic or coupon-based.
  4. Review order limits and trial terms on the App Store pricing section.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best Shopify checkout gift app?

The best fit depends on whether you need only a fixed gift or a full checkout stack (gifts, upsells, trust, payment tweaks). Compare features on the Shopify App Store and test on a development store before launch.

Do checkout gift apps require Shopify Plus?

Native checkout UI extensions for gifts and upsells typically require Shopify Plus. Thank You and Order Status content widgets may be available on all plans — verify on each app listing.

How is Smart Checkout Widgets priced?

Starter is free to install on the Shopify App Store with order limits; Growth and Advanced are paid tiers with trials. See structured pricing and the public listing for current numbers.
