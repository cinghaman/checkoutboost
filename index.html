FAQ

Questions merchants ask before they install.

What is Smart Checkout Widgets focused on now?

The product story should center on checkout gifting, especially automatic gifting and the option for customers to choose their own gift when merchants configure multiple gift items.

Can the app do more than gifting?

Yes. The app also supports upsells, trust badges, custom banners, custom content, payment icons, consent checks, and other checkout merchandising elements.

What pricing should the homepage communicate?

The public pricing story should be visible on-page: Starter is free to install, Growth is $29 per month, and Advanced is $59 per month, with a 7-day free trial on paid tiers.

Why add blog content to the marketing site?

Blog pages give you direct landing pages for feature-specific search intent, support internal linking, and let you talk about new product updates without overloading the homepage.
